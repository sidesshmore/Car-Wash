Our Process

Water Jet Washing

Car is subjected to high speed water
ejectors to remove dust/debris from every nook & corner.

Shampoo Washing

Lather created using oil/dust
absorbing shampoo is sprayed over the entire car using hose pipe.

Hydraulic Lift Washing

Car is lifted up using hydraulic car lift
to clean the lower areas of the car which aren't visible.

Robotic Washing

Fully automatic robotic arm is used
to sponge-clean your car and apply shiny coating.
